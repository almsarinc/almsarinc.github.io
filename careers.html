AI Integration Engineer

Location: Remote-first (Canada or U.S. preferred)

Type: Full-time · Contract-to-hire options available

About the Role

We’re looking for an AI Integration Engineer to help design, implement, and support practical machine learning solutions integrated into real-world business software. You’ll work with our development team to bridge modern AI tools with Django-based APIs and enterprise systems.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and integrate ML models into production Django applications
  • Build REST APIs that expose AI services to internal and client systems
  • Fine-tune and optimize models using PyTorch or similar frameworks
  • Collaborate with frontend and backend teams to ensure seamless UX
  • Document systems clearly for internal and external users
  • Help evaluate trade-offs between traditional and AI-driven solutions

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience with Python in a production environment
  • Hands-on experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow
  • Strong familiarity with Django and Django REST Framework
  • Experience integrating 3rd-party APIs and LLM services (e.g., OpenAI, HuggingFace)
  • Understanding of software engineering principles, version control (git), and CI/CD practices
  • Ability to work independently and communicate clearly

Nice to Have

  • Experience with prompt engineering or RAG pipelines
  • Familiarity with containerized deployments (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Experience in enterprise or regulated environments

To Apply

Email your resume and a brief intro to careers@almsarinc.com. Include links to any relevant code, GitHub profiles, or past work you’re proud of.
